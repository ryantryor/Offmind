# Estimation isn't lying, but it's not accurate either

Ranges, not points. 'It'll take 1-3 days' is honest; '2.5 days' is fake precision. Estimate the ideal-engineering-time, then multiply by 2 for meetings, code review, debugging unknowns. Track your accuracy weekly to calibrate.
