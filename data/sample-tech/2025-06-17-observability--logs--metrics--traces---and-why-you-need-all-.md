# Observability: logs, metrics, traces - and why you need all three

Logs: discrete events, expensive to query at scale. Metrics: aggregated, cheap, no high-cardinality dimensions. Traces: request flow across services, finds latency causes. OpenTelemetry now standardizes all three - instrument once, ship to Datadog/Honeycomb/whatever.
