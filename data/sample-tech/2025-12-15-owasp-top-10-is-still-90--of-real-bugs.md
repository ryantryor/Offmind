# OWASP top 10 is still 90% of real bugs

Injection (SQL, command), broken auth, sensitive data exposure, XXE, broken access control, security misconfig, XSS, insecure deserialization, vulnerable components, insufficient logging. Most security incidents I've seen at small companies: hardcoded secrets, missing access control checks, or shipping a version of a library with a known CVE.
