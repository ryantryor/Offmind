# Why I switched from Notion to local markdown

Notion's nice UX, but: data lock-in, slow on big workspaces, no offline, search is mediocre. Plain markdown in a folder + Obsidian/VS Code: portable, fast, scriptable. Lost: collaboration, embeds, databases. Gained: speed, ownership, longevity.
