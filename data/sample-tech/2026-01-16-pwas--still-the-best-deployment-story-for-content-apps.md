# PWAs: still the best deployment story for content apps

One codebase, no app store, automatic updates, full URL-based deep linking. Limits: limited push notifications on iOS, no native APIs (Bluetooth, NFC, deep system access). If your app is content-heavy and doesn't need native APIs, PWA is faster to build and easier to maintain.
