# State management 2026: when you actually need Redux

For most apps in 2026: useState + Context is enough. Add Zustand or Jotai when you need cross-tree state. Reach for Redux only when you have: complex middleware needs, time-travel debugging requirements, or a team already trained on it. Don't add it speculatively.
