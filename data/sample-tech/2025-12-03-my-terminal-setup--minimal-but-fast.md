# My terminal setup: minimal but fast

Wezterm + zsh + starship + fzf + zoxide + ripgrep + bat. Total config is ~100 lines. Aliases I actually use: gst (git status), gd (git diff), gco (checkout), . (cd), ll (ls -lh). Custom keybindings: ctrl-r for fzf history search, ctrl-t for fzf file picker.
