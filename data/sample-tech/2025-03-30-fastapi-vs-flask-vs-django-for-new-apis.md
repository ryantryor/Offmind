# FastAPI vs Flask vs Django for new APIs

FastAPI: pydantic validation, async-first, auto OpenAPI docs. Best default for new APIs. Flask: minimal, flexible, sync-only. Good for tiny services. Django: full-stack with ORM, admin, auth out of the box. Best when you need a CMS-like backend.
