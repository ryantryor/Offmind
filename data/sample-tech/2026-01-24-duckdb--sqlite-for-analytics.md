# DuckDB: SQLite for analytics

Single-binary, embedded OLAP database. Reads Parquet/CSV/JSON directly, no ETL needed. Crushes Pandas on 10M+ row aggregations. Use for: ad-hoc analysis, ETL pipelines, embedded analytics in apps. Replace Pandas + SQLAlchemy + plotting glue with one tool.
