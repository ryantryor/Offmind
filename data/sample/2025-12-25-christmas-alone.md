Christmas Day. Alone at home.

A year ago I would have called this a failure. Tonight it just feels quiet.

Boiled a bowl of frozen dumplings. Watched an old movie. Video-called Mom; she held up her new potted plant to the camera and made me admire it for a full minute.

At nine I sat by the window and watched the lights across the street. Every window is a story, a person, a family. I'm one of them too.

I don't know what next year will look like. But tonight, I'm at peace. That's enough.
