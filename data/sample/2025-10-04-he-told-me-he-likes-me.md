A Zhe told me last night he likes me. We've known each other almost a year and I never saw it coming.

I didn't answer right away. I told him I needed a little time. He said, "no rush."

I thought about it all day. Do I like him? I honestly don't know. I love being around him — it feels safe, no performing. But from that kind of liking to this kind of liking, there's a distance.

I told myself: don't say yes just because you're scared of missing out. Don't say no just because you're scared of getting hurt.

Go slowly. If there's one thing I learned this year, it's that going slowly is fine.
