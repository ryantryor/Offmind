title: "Back to the town I grew up in"
date: 2025-08-05
category: travel

lang: en

Went back to my hometown for three days.

The street I used to walk to school — every shop on it has changed. The stationery store became a bubble tea place. The arcade became a real estate agency. Only the old crooked elm tree is still where it was.

I stood under that tree for a long time. My head full of 12-year-old me, backpack on, eating a 50-cent popsicle, thinking the world was vast and without edges.

On the drive back home it hit me: a hometown isn't a place, it's a span of time. And that span is gone, because I'm not who I was then.

But that's okay. Remembering is enough.
