title: "Third day at my parents' place"
date: 2024-08-03
category: family
tags: [family, happiness, memory]
lang: en
---

Third day home. Mom asks me every morning what I want for dinner, then makes four dishes I didn't ask for. Dad sits on the balcony smoking, and every time he sees me he says, "work been tiring?"

We watched TV together tonight. Some ancient drama they've seen fifteen times. I was lying on the couch, Mom leaning against me knitting, Dad dozing off beside her.

It hit me suddenly — this is the thing I'm actually protecting when I stay up late, when I get angry at coworkers, when I spiral over KPIs. This quiet, ordinary eight-o'clock living room.

I want to remember tonight.
