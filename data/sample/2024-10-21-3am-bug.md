3am. Finally fixed the bug that's been haunting me for a week.

It was a race condition hiding between two async calls, only firing under specific production traffic. Couldn't repro it locally. The logs were useless.

I stared at the screen and read every possibly-relevant line of code. On the fourth pass, I saw it. Just sitting there. It had been there the whole time.

After the fix went out I didn't go straight to bed. I made tea and opened the window. The autumn wind came in, cold. I felt alive. I haven't felt that in a long time.
