title: "They dissolved our team"
date: 2025-04-28
category: work
tags: [work, disappointment, drift]

---

Leadership announced today that our whole team is being "reorganized." They phrased it beautifully — "strategic realignment," "improving org efficiency." What they meant: product direction changed and the thing we spent a year building is no longer important.

I stared at the leadership faces on Zoom and suddenly couldn't remember what the last eight months of overtime were for.

After work I didn't go home right away. Sat in the café downstairs, ordered a black coffee, watched the window.

Not crying. Not angry. Just — tired. A deep, bone-level tired.
