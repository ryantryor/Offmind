Told Xiao Yu tonight that it was over. Three years.

It wasn't a fight. Nobody did anything wrong. It's just — we both knew the relationship had been slowly dying for a while, and neither of us wanted to say it first. She cried. I didn't. But when I got home I sat on the couch without moving for two hours.

There's still a carton of strawberries in the fridge she bought last time. I don't have the courage to throw them out. I don't have the courage to eat them either.

Tomorrow I have to go to work and pretend everything is normal.
